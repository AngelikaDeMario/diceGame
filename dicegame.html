The Unforgivable Rules
Rule 1: There are six levels: Hogwarts, The Chamber of Secrets, Azkaban, Triwizard Maze, Ministry of Magic, and The Tower.
Rule #2: Each level will increase with difficulty.
Rule #3: If you get the question wrong, you stay on the level and roll again until the question is answered correctly.

Good luck, 'Until the very end'.
